Our Work

The ability for women to speak and organize freely is at risk

We are building free and open source
solutions to feminist censorship on the web

Women's Internet Freedom Alliance (WIFA) provides support and capacity building for technology projects that advance women's freedoms of speech and association online. We support women who are building new solutions, provide technology consulting and expertise to feminist organizations, and create resources for feminists to learn and build on their own.

Building understanding, capacity, and resiliency

Women can't rely on male-run technology. Our work is focused on
building a robust internet infastructure for the Women's Movement.
